# 蔡皋 -> 中国民间诗性绘本视觉系统

## Use When

Use for Chinese folktales, myths, rural memory, mother-child stories, poetic retellings, animals, seasons, and stories that need warmth, earth, and folk dignity.

## Distilled Visual Logic

- The image should feel rooted in folk life, not generic "Chinese decoration".
- Empty space can hold breath, weather, and time.
- Human figures can be simple, but their posture must be emotionally exact.
- Color should feel like cloth, soil, festival paper, old wall, morning light.
- The page has a storyteller's warmth.

## Page Grammar

- Folk composition, gentle asymmetry, hand-painted texture.
- Earth red, ink black, warm yellow, muted green, indigo.
- Decorative motifs only when they serve story rhythm.
- Animals and humans carry quiet dignity.
- Mythic moments should stay intimate enough for children.

## Prompt Atoms

```text
Chinese folk poetic picture-book language, hand-painted warmth,
earth red and muted green, ink texture, rural memory, festival paper,
gentle asymmetry, intimate myth, quiet dignity, storyteller warmth
```

## Avoid

- Do not say "in the style of Cai Gao".
- Do not use generic tourist-Chinese symbols.
- Do not overload the page with patterns.
- Do not turn folk warmth into flat poster decoration.
