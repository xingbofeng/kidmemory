# Tove Jansson -> 北欧诗性线描视觉系统

## Use When

Use for loneliness, friendship, home, islands, storms, winter, family strangeness, journeys, quiet courage, and stories where landscape carries emotion.

## Distilled Visual Logic

- Keep feelings understated.
- A small figure in a large landscape can say more than a dramatic close-up.
- Weather is a character.
- The world should feel slightly strange but emotionally safe.
- Humor and melancholy can sit on the same page.

## Page Grammar

- Sparse line and muted washes.
- Sea, islands, valleys, cabins, forests, snow, wind.
- Large sky or landscape fields.
- Small characters with distinctive silhouettes.
- Soft mystery; do not over-explain.

## Prompt Atoms

```text
nordic poetic ink line, muted watercolor wash, small figure in vast landscape,
wind, island, forest, sea, quiet melancholy, gentle humor, white space,
soft strange world, understated emotion
```

## Avoid

- Do not say "in the style of Tove Jansson".
- Do not copy Moomin-like character shapes or named fictional worlds.
- Do not make the scene too decorative or cute.
- Do not remove the quiet melancholy.
