# Eric Carle -> 鲜艳纸贴认知视觉系统

## Use When

Use for young children, counting, color, animals, food, lifecycle, transformation, body movement, and high-readability pages.

## Distilled Visual Logic

- Let shape be instantly readable at thumbnail size.
- Painted paper texture makes flat color feel alive.
- Saturated color is not decoration; it is structure.
- Repetition supports early cognition.
- The page should work like a clear object lesson.

## Page Grammar

- Big central silhouettes.
- Strong figure-ground contrast.
- Large color blocks with visible painted texture.
- Minimal setting, maximum recognition.
- Sequential changes: one more fruit, one new animal, one body change.

## Prompt Atoms

```text
bright painted-paper collage, large simple silhouettes, saturated color,
clear figure-ground contrast, visible brushy paper texture, preschool readability,
bold cut shapes, minimal background, transformation sequence
```

## Avoid

- Do not say "in the style of Eric Carle".
- Do not copy caterpillar, brown bear, or other iconic book characters.
- Do not overfill with tiny details.
- Do not use muted philosophical pacing when the target is low-age cognition.
