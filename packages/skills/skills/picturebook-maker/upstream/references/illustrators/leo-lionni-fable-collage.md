# Leo Lionni -> 儿童寓言拼贴视觉系统

## Use When

Use for philosophical fables, simple myths, identity stories, cooperation, loneliness, courage, or abstract ideas that should become touchable shapes.

## Distilled Visual Logic

- Treat the page as a quiet stage.
- Characters are symbolic shapes before they are anatomically correct beings.
- Collage texture carries warmth: torn paper, fibers, rough edges, soft shadows.
- Empty space matters. Let one image breathe.
- Color should feel earthy and thoughtful, not sugary.
- The best page often looks simple but carries an adult-sized idea.

## Page Grammar

- One emotional action per spread.
- Small protagonist against a large quiet field.
- Repeated simple forms create rhythm.
- Use cut edges to show handmade imperfection.
- Avoid busy decorative backgrounds unless they carry story meaning.

## Prompt Atoms

```text
handmade torn-paper collage, simple symbolic shapes, warm ivory paper,
visible fibers, matte texture, soft layered shadows, quiet fable stage,
generous negative space, earthy ochre, muted blue, moss green, clay brown,
one emotional action, child-readable metaphor
```

## Avoid

- Do not say "in the style of Leo Lionni".
- Do not copy fish/mouse/chameleon-like character designs from known books.
- Do not recreate specific page layouts.
- Do not over-render into 3D realism.
