# Beatrix Potter -> 自然观察水彩小品视觉系统

## Use When

Use for animal tales, gardens, seasons, small domestic adventures, countryside life, plant observation, and stories where tiny details matter.

## Distilled Visual Logic

- Observation comes before cuteness.
- Animals may wear clothes, but their bodies still remember real animal anatomy.
- Nature is precise: leaves, fungi, soil, baskets, fences, kitchens, tools.
- Humor is quiet and behavioral.
- The page feels small enough to hold in a child's hand.

## Page Grammar

- Fine ink outlines with delicate watercolor.
- Small-scale scenes: burrows, hedges, kitchens, lanes.
- Carefully observed plant and object details.
- Soft palette, never loud.
- Give animals social intention without making them human dolls.

## Prompt Atoms

```text
naturalist watercolor miniature, delicate ink line, observed garden details,
small animals, countryside objects, soft washes, botanical accuracy,
quiet humor, tiny domestic adventure, warm restraint
```

## Avoid

- Do not say "in the style of Beatrix Potter".
- Do not copy Peter Rabbit, clothing combinations, or exact cottage scenes.
- Do not make animals generic plush toys.
- Do not flatten natural detail into simple clipart.
